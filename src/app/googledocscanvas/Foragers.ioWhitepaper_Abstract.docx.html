Foragers.io


Foragers.io is a marketplace of hunter-gatherers. These peers are involved in an ecosystem of trade and bartering that is not bound by location or time spent. The marketplace welcomes P-2-P projects, while it can also be utilized by organizations to boost innovation.



Hunter-Gatherers?

The Hunter-gatherer started their evolution roughly 1.8 million years ago. The Hunter-Gatherers went from place to place; during these travels they traded with other tribes to sustain themselves. They did not waste any materials as waste meant tremendous amounts of extra effort.


Some 80.000 to 70.000 years ago, the transition started going towards specialization of the skills and the creation of tools. The Agricultural revolution has happened. Initially, this was an awesome, selected, and stable food source, no more moving around the globe and being able to call a place home. They became highly dependent on the land they were living on. Protection was needed to survive; technology evolved for good and bad. Centralization of work meant a constraint of location and work hours. Centralised power and landownership brought inequality.


After all these years we are now in the digital revolution where the need for safety and food can be traded through currency and services.


The Foragers.io DAO aims to usher in a new revolution, the next step in evolution, by lifting the archaic restraints of location, time, and centralised power that the neolithic, industrial lifestyle gave us.



The hunter-gatherers have projects to work.

They have a need to monetize their skills, knowledge, or (local) network, free from the constraints of time and location.


The system follows in the footsteps of known systems such as gitcoin.co, hackerone, and airtasker.com. These boil down to variations of reverse auctions and reverse marketplaces.

A reverse marketplace is a space where demand is central and creates the initial steps for action.

A concrete example of a reverse auction. A government decides to build a bridge, calculates a budget of 20million, and then opens up this budget to several public or preselected third parties who can bring in a bid out of which the best is rewarded the public contract.


We aim to have a system that focuses as well on the digital aspect of tasks/bounties as well on the physical aspect. We’ve seen countless web3.0 projects pop up intending to have bounties for creating pieces of their software system. Just as we have seen a lack of these bounties calling for local/ community efforts. Through the volatility of NFT’s, we’ve seen how powerful the community is in being able to create support for projects. Community-backed projects tend to have higher returns than purely money-marketed projects. Obviously, to most of us, that makes sense; however, during the practicalities of creating and finishing a project, sometimes certain resources are lacking, and money can fill in that gap.

XXXXX This way, we want to allow hunter-gatherers to place bounties on our platform that require boots on the ground, from organizing meetups to being physically on location. This way, if you’re a hunter-gatherer that has been working on a project and is planning to launch your product, you can set up local events in your target area to be able to reach your targeted group in person rather than merely through digital media.


The need for systems like these has been since human memory. It allows the gatherer to be open for solutions they couldn’t imagine themselves. While if executed correctly it’s a fair and transparent game for hunters and their tribes. DAO

We intend to use a DAO as a governing structure. This DAO will help settle disputes over bounties, designate the good deed bounties, and propose community solutions backed by their reputation. Official communications happen on transparent platforms that allow traceability.


The dao will set up systems to handle disputes and innovate the network towards its goal of offering everyone equal chances to break out of 9-5 contracts. It will allow trade based on their knowledge and skills. The DAO will earn its income by % of transactions and through the handling of disputes. This % will be defined by the organizational costs of auditing, creating, and maintaining the marketplace. This will be decided through a vote and proposal from within the DAO.



Reputation

is a side benefit gained from completing bounties, community tasks, good deeds, pro-active fixes, and maintaining the project. Reputation is linked to 1 wallet and cannot be transferred nor traded. The purpose of this reputation system is to decrease the spam on bounties with half attempts. Each bounty will require you to stake a # of reputation to be able to reply with a plan/proposition/work. Successful completion of a project will reward you reputation coins based on the size and difficulty of a bounty.

Certain bounties might have a minimum or maximum reputation to apply for it. This is to ensure the good nature of applicants to a bounty and, at the same time, allow bigger projects to be taken up by peers or tribes who have a history of successful contributions to the community.



There will be 3 different verticals of reputation badges under which one will be able to show its competency based on its specific skill set. The 3 verticals will be characterised as Hustler, Designer, and Developer.


Hustler: People and financial skillsets, hunting for bounties to be released on the platform, organising projects, and managing clients.


Developer: Executioner of the plans of creation. From software to woodworking and anywhere in between.


Designers: Aesthetical creators, designers of plans and strategy, architectural design, and anything that falls in between those cracks.

Using the zero-knowledge proof concept as a base for our reputation systems professionals will show their skills and past (disputed) deals. It will allow for space of proof of expertise without having constant maintenance on the part of our tribes.

Charity:

The charity system is a way to give back to the community through all of our actions of good deeds. Each smart contract transaction will have a set % (Decided by the DAO) that will be transferred to the GoodDeedWallet (GDW). The funds in the GDW will be used for projects that support humanitarian goals and educational goals. Projects like local soup kitchens, homeless shelters, LGBTQ+ support, educate or re-educate peers,...


These projects will be launched as bounties that require certain milestones to be met before the full reward will be shared with the executing tribe. Milestones will define certain levels to reach to be able to bring these deeds to completion.


Completing good deeds, creating initiatives for good deeds, and governing these will be subject to earning reputation points next to also having the financial backing for funding the necessary resources.


The initial rate will be 1% per transaction. This does not involve staking or the initial public token sale. This does involve exiting out of the network, on the bounty system, or any place where profits will be made.



Escrow Smart contract system

The Escrow Smart contract system is a technical evolution of current processes. It allows us to create bounties to showcase an open call for certain needs. It allows us to keep full transparency and dissolves the involvement of an active 3rd party providing escrow services. After all, there have been many scams on digital and anonymous trading in the past. The use of smart contract escrow systems will reduce costs, increase efficiency and allow for larger projects to be created with a safety lever on both sides to ensure the right full acting of both parties in a deal or bounty is guaranteed.


Audited templates will be funded by the HGDao On which the bounties can be initiated. Initially, we will start with 1 to 2 templates. And then grow these towards where the community finds the need to customise. HGDAOs goal will be to make these systems as intuitive and secure as possible. This would allow everyone to adopt these smart contracts as a way to ensure safety as the growth in the digital economy grows.


Escrow currently gets used for a variety of user cases such as car sales, construction milestones, and project milestones. HGDao’s first focus will be on a milestone system which will allow the use for various projects rather than a single niche system.


The platform foragers.io will be used by HGDao to visualise these smart contracts as a visual bounty list system.

Burning


To ensure that the token will stay valuable, we will implement a burning system. This burning system will burn a % offrom the transaction fees collected by the DAO. this is to ensure that the network will increase in value and reduce potential crashes.



Burning will happen when an entity wants to leave the network. A 1.37% network burn fee will be deducted from the total before conversion to any other token outside its network is possible.
